The Great Deku Tree
In the Kokiri Forest, Link is summoned by the Great Deku Tree. In order to prove his courage, Link must destroy the Great Deku Tree's curse...
Becoming the Hero!
Despite his lack of training, Link bravely ventures inside the Great Deku Tree and defeats his enemies!
Saving the Deku Tree!
Eventually, Link defeats the dungeon boss and obtains the first spiritual stone!
Meeting Zelda
After leaving the Kokiri Forest, Link meets Princess Zelda at Hyrule Castle. She commands Link to obtain the Master Sword and defeat Ganondorf.
No Time Left!
Upon returning to Hyrule Castle, Link meets Ganondorf, who is far too powerful for him right now...
The Master Sword
Link hurries to the Temple of Time and pulls the Master Sword, but unexpectedly enters a 7-year slumber!
Reunion with Epona
After waking up, Link is now an adult. Hyrule is no longer the same, but he still has Epona
Meeting Sheik
During his journey, Link meets Sheik, who assists him to defeat Ganondorf. Little does he know, that Sheik is actually Zelda in disguise...
Freeing the Sages
Slowly but surely, Link becomes stronger by defeating enemies and freeing the legendary sages!
The Final Battle!
After freeing all five sages, Link finally defeats Ganondrof in one final battle!
Peace Restored
After saving Hyrule, Link returns to the past and makes up for lost time
